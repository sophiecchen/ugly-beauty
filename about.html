Ugly Beauty is an informational site that addresses pollution and plastic waste in the beauty industry. The beauty industry is full of environmental problems, from its plastic packaging and toxic ingredients to its encouragement of consumerism. I wanted to inspire users to take a closer look their consumer habits and the society they live in. The issues within the beauty industry are just one part of a society heading toward environmental ruin. If the beauty industry causes so much environmental destruction by itself, then how much harm do all industries cause together?
