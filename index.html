Makeup Hides an Ugly Truth

↓

Behind the glitz and glam, the beauty industry leaves an ugly footprint on the environment. Makeup can come with poisonous materials, from the plastic in packaging to toxic ingredients, that can destroy the environment, especially marine life.

Plastic Packaging

Glitter

Toxic Ingredients

Both consumers and companies are at fault. While many consumers buy excessive amounts of product, which produces excess waste, companies use greenwashing to trick buyers rather than actually providing eco-friendly options.

Consumer Culture

Company Irresponsibility

Instead of buying makeup packaged in plastic, buy fewer, refillable products that you love. Hold companies accountable by only purchasing from ethical, eco-friendly brands and advocating for the environmentally-friendly practices.
